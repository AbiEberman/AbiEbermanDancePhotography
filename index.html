About

Where art
meets lens.

I'm Abi Eberman — a dance photographer with over a decade of experience working with ballet companies, contemporary ensembles, and independent artists around the world.

My work lives in the space between stillness and motion. Each image is an attempt to translate the language of the body — its strength, its vulnerability, its poetry — into something that endures long after the music fades.

I work closely with each artist to understand the story they are telling, so that every frame is not just a photograph, but a collaboration.
